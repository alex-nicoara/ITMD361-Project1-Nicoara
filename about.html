About Me

Introduction

My name is Alexander Nicoara. I am currently a 5th year Computer Science major at Illinois Institute of Technology specializing in Web Development. My hometown is Elk Grove Village, a northwest suburb of Chicago, but I currently reside in Chicago during the school year. While on campus, I am involved with Cru and my Fraternity (Phi Kappa Sigma). Both of these organizations have helped me grow professionally and personally, leading me to be more organized, social, and personable. As a student, I am enrolled in classes pertaining to web design, operating systems, probability/statistics, and graphics rendering protocol. I am eager to finish my last semester of school before heading off into the real world.

Mission

As a web developer, my mission is to produce graphically appealing and user-friendly websites/web-apps for customers. I tend to put myself into the user's shoes and try to examine their thought process as they are interacting with the web content. The design I choose must be simple for the user, easy to build upon, and secure for both the client and the host. I believe these 3 components are the key to creating a great website/web application. As a consumer myself, this is what I generally expect when browsing/interacting with a website/web-app.
